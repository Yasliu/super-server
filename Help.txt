For connecting to the Database, you need to paste this in the
dependencies section of the pom.xml file:

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.xerial</groupId>
    <artifactId>sqlite-jdbc</artifactId>
    <version>3.46.0.0</version>
</dependency>

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-data-jpa</artifactId>
</dependency>

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.hibernate.orm</groupId>
    <artifactId>hibernate-community-dialects</artifactId>
</dependency>

<dependency>
    <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
    <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-thymeleaf</artifactId>
</dependency>

Afterwards you need to go to the application.properties and add this:

-- Ensure that your .db is in the same folder as your main folder--
spring.datasource.url=jdbc:sqlite:gradebook.db
spring.datasource.driver-class-name=org.sqlite.JDBC

-- This is the dialect for SQLite--
spring.jpa.database-platform=org.hibernate.community.dialect.SQLiteDialect

-- This is to show the SQL queries in the console--
spring.jpa.show-sql=true

spring.jpa.hibernate.ddl-auto=update
